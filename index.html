My Journey to Web Development

Hello, my name is Leah Nelson, and I am a Web Developer. Originally from Miami, Fl and I now call Chicago, IL Home ( although I question that decision every February). Creating custom web soultions for clients that soon become friends is what I enjoy and how I pay for my travel addiction. Ok, I also use the money to pay for boring things like rent, food, and student loans. My specialty includes Wordpress and Divi, MEAN Stack, Javascript, Bootstrap, and the basics such as HTML and CSS. My previous professional life in Sales and Marketing benefits my clients, not only do I make beautiful web things but I understand what the purpose of those things should be. I beleive in professional solutions to problems, and bringing ideas to life, while enjoying life. Feel free to browse around my portfolio page and contact me if you have an idea for some web things.
