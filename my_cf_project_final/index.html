FAQs

Answers to the Frequently Asked Questions

What is your work style?

Typically a client will approach with an idea for a project, my first step is to listen, by listening I gain an understanding of not only what the client wants but what they need. I have a very collaborative work style and with scheduled frequent checkpoints, and contious flow of feedback my clients find my style open, professional and collaborative.

Why did you decide to become a Web Developer?

The decision to become a Web Developer did not come lightly. After several years of various jobs from bartending, to customer service supervisor for an auto insurance company. I realized that they all had a few things in common, lack of passion on my part, and they were just jobs not a career ( for me anyway.) Web Development is one of the few things that I would learn and experiment with for free, when I realized that I could get paid to play and create I was sold. I formally began my education and training with CareerFoundry in March of 2015 and have never regretted making the choice.

What is your average project turnaround?

Time from initial client meeting to project delivery is based upon each individual project. The clearer the direction of the project is the quicker the turnaround will be.
